Arcane

Watch Now

Overview

Arcane is an animated series set in the universe of the popular video game League of Legends. It explores the origins of iconic characters and the delicate balance between the wealthy city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun.

Key Information

  • Genre: Animation, Action, Adventure
  • Release Date: November 6, 2021
  • Seasons: 1 (with a second season confirmed)
  • Episodes: 9

Cast & Creative Team

Creators: Christian Linke, Alex Yee

Main Cast:

  • Hailee Steinfeld as Vi
  • Ella Purnell as Jinx
  • Kevin Alejandro as Jayce
  • Jason Spisak as Silco

Characters

Arcane features a rich cast of characters, including:

  • Vi: A fierce fighter with a troubled past.
  • Jinx: A chaotic and unpredictable criminal.
  • Jayce: A brilliant inventor striving for progress.
  • Silco: A cunning antagonist with a vision for Zaun.

Reviews

Arcane has received critical acclaim for its animation style, storytelling, and character development. It holds a 100% rating on Rotten Tomatoes, with critics praising its emotional depth and visual artistry.
